My journey to code started at a DC Startup Weekend conference. Everyone already had a snazzy, cool business idea. The real demand was to find a talented developer for their team.

I started to do online tutorials in coding. I took a Java Programming class. I joined and became an organizer for DC Rails Girls. And I went through a coding bootcamp.

My interests are far-reaching. From improv, travel, Earth Science, Religion, Diplomacy and Journalism, I am always looking for new initiatives to learn more and to contribute.

Per my curiosity and affinity to say 'yes' to new challenges, I have my own company doing research for authors. My 'can do' attitude landed me in business operations. Now, I am also a web developer.
